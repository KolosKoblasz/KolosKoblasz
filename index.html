Xilinx FPGA online report parser

This site parses your FPGA project's resource utilization report

First build your project successfuly!

Then drop here your Vivado project's interesting files:

  1. "YourProjectFolder/project.runs/impl_n/top_utilization_placed.rpt"
  2. "YourProjectFolder/project.runs/impl_n/top_timing_summary_routed.rpt"
